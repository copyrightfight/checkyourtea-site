Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At CheckYourTea, your privacy is our top priority. We understand the sensitive nature of our services and are committed to protecting your personal information with the highest level of discretion and security.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum information necessary to provide our search services:

  • Search Details: Names, locations, and other identifying information you provide for the purpose of the search.
  • Contact Information: Your email address or preferred contact method to deliver results.
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe; we do not store your credit card details on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • Conducting the requested Tea App search.
  • Communicating with you regarding your order.
  • Delivering the search results and video recordings.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

We believe in "privacy by deletion":

  • Search Results: Video recordings of searches are password-protected and automatically deleted within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Immediate Deletion: You may request the immediate deletion of your search data and results at any time after delivery.
  • No Permanent Profiles: We do not build profiles on our users or the subjects of their searches.

4. Confidentiality

All searches are conducted anonymously and leave no trace. Your identity and the fact that a search was performed remain completely confidential. We do not share your information with third parties, except as required by law or to process payments via our secure provider.

5. Self-Search Privacy

For our Self-Search Service, we provide a secure, private workspace. This session is encrypted and completely wiped once your search is finished, ensuring no record of your activity remains.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at privacy@checkyourtea.com.
